Card Type Selection & Input Guide

Question Separator: ///

MCQ Format: question|A|B|C|D|E|F|correctAnswer
• Support for 4 or 5 choices
• Correct answer in the last column

Basic Format: question|correctAnswer
• Question in first column
• Answer in second column

Cloze Deletion Format: text with {{c1::hidden}} parts
• Cloze text in first column

Examples:
MCQ (4 choices): What is Paris?///France|Italy|Spain|Germany///A
MCQ (5 choices): What is the capital?///Paris|London|Berlin|Madrid|Amsterdam///A
Basic: France|Capital of France
Cloze: {{c1::Anthropic}} is an AI company founded in {{c2::2021}}.
